Pila Goenpo Wangyal

Pila Goenpo Wangyel was born in 1782. Later he went to Dungkar and married Jangsa Sonam Pelzom and gave birth to five children.The history discloses that the name of the place “Dungkar” was originated from the physical shape of the land. The place bears the exact shape of a conch shell. Dungkar Choeje Lhakhang is believed to be stand on the tip of the Dungkar like shape of the land.Dungkar village is the ancestral home town of our beloved monarch whereby Dungkar Choeje Lhakhang is the birth place of our “ Wangchuck Dynasty”.

When Dungsay Kuenga Gyeltshen was in meditation in Kuenzangling , his Sangyum gave birth to five sons. In third year, his three sons known as Phola Phuen Sum. Thereafter, his three sons Pho La Puen Sum visited Dungkar and resided there. Thereafter Dungkar was known as Dungkar Norbu Choling.
